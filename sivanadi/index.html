Shiva Nadi Astrology

Ancient Palm Leaf Divine Predictions

What is Shiva Nadi?

Shiva Nadi is an ancient palm leaf astrology system believed to be written under the divine blessings of Lord Shiva. Great sages recorded human destinies thousands of years ago on sacred palm leaves.

How Does It Work?

The reading begins with a thumb impression. Based on this, palm leaf bundles are searched and matched. Once identified, life predictions including marriage, career, health and remedies are explained.

Spiritual Importance

Shiva Nadi provides guidance to understand karma and receive spiritual remedies to overcome life obstacles.
