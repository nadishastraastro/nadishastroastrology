Astrology History

Vaitheeswaran Koil Nadi Astrology is one of the most popular and respected types of Nadi astrology in India. It started at the Vaitheeswaran Koil Temple in Tamil Nadu. This special system uses your thumbprint to match you with a unique palm leaf that holds detailed information about your life. Nadi astrologers study your thumbprint to find the right leaf, which shares details about your past, present, and future. Known for its accuracy and deep spiritual meaning, this method is highly trusted for its precise predictions.

Introduction to Nadi Astrology

Nadi Astrology is one of the most mysterious and sacred branches of ancient Indian astrology. According to traditional belief, great Rishi Munis thousands of years ago predicted the life details of millions of souls and recorded them on palm leaves known as Nadi Granthas.

Agastya (Agasthiyar Rishi)

Agastya Maharishi is the most famous Nadi Astrology writer. He is believed to have received divine knowledge through meditation and spiritual powers (Jnana Drishti). Agastya Nadi is highly popular in Tamil Nadu and widely used for marriage, career, business, health, and karmic remedies.

Bhrigu Maharishi

Bhrigu Rishi is associated with the Bhrigu Samhita. His predictions focus on planetary influences, karma-based life analysis, and Vedic astrology principles.

Shukra Maharishi

Shukra Maharishi is connected to wealth, marriage, prosperity, and financial remedies. His guidance is highly sought for love problems, delayed marriage, and financial stability.

Vasistha Maharishi

Vasistha Maharishi, one of the Saptarishis, focused on family karma, dharma, spiritual upliftment, and remedies through prayers and homam.
