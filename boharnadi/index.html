Bohar Nadi is one of the ancient branches of Nadi Jyotish, a sacred palm leaf astrology system preserved in Tamil Nadi Shastra tradition. It is believed that enlightened sages recorded detailed human destinies on palm leaves thousands of years ago.

Bohar Nadi Astrology focuses on major life transformations, karmic influences, marriage timing, career growth, wealth accumulation, and spiritual remedies.

Unlike general horoscope astrology, Bohar Nadi Palm Leaf Reading provides highly specific and personal life details such as:

  • Name and family identification details
  • Marriage timing and spouse information
  • Career development and job opportunities
  • Business success and financial growth
  • Health concerns and solutions
  • Karma-based spiritual remedies (Pariharam)
