Horoscope

A horoscope is an astrological chart showing the positions of celestial bodies—like the Sun, Moon, planets, and stars—at a specific time, usually the exact moment of a person’s birth. It is used to interpret a person’s personality, behavior, strengths, weaknesses, and future events.

How Horoscope Analysis Works

1️⃣ Step 1: Collect Birth Details

To create an accurate horoscope, you need:

  • Date of Birth
  • Exact Time of Birth
  • Place of Birth

2️⃣ Step 2: Create the Birth Chart (Kundali / Natal Chart)

Planets are placed in the houses according to their positions at birth.

3️⃣ Step 3: Analyze Zodiac Signs and Planetary Positions

Each planet governs certain traits or areas of life:

  • Sun → Ego, vitality
  • Moon → Emotions, mind
  • Mars → Energy, courage
  • Mercury → Communication, intellect
  • Jupiter → Wisdom, wealth
  • Venus → Love, relationships
  • Saturn → Discipline, challenges
  • Rahu & Ketu → Karma, spiritual lessons

4️⃣ Step 4: Look at Planetary Relationships (Aspects & Yogas)

Planets affect each other based on their angles and positions in the chart.

Certain combinations (Yogas) are considered favorable or unfavorable, affecting:

  • Wealth
  • Relationships
  • Health
  • Career

5️⃣ Step 5: Make Predictions

Using the positions of planets, houses, and their relationships, astrologers interpret:

  • Personality traits
  • Career path
  • Love and marriage
  • Health and wellbeing
  • Future opportunities and challenges
