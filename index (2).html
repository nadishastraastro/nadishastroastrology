Vastu Shastra (Vastu) Definition

Vastu Shastra is an ancient Indian science of architecture, design, and spatial arrangement that aims to harmonize human dwellings with natural forces. The term comes from Sanskrit:

  • Vastu = dwelling, building, or structure
  • Shastra = science or knowledge

So, Vastu Shastra literally means “the science of building or architecture.” It provides guidelines for designing homes, offices, temples, and other structures in a way that aligns with natural energies like sunlight, wind, water, and the earth’s magnetic field, aiming to create a balanced, positive, and prosperous environment.
