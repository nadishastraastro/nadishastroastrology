Nadi Astrology is an ancient Tamil system of astrology. This astrology is performed based on palm-leaf manuscripts written thousands of years ago by great sages.

These palm leaves are believed to have been written by sages like Agastya and Bhrigu.

The world-famous Nadi center is located in Tamil Nadu at Vaitheeswaran Koil. It is situated in Mayiladuthurai district.
